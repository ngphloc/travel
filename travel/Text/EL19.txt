Located at 28 Vo Van Tan St. in District 3, this is the most popular modern museum. It contains many photographs, map, and exhibits such as arms, boms, chemical defoliants, helicopters, tanks, etc. Many os the photographs are ftom Western news service and were well publicised in the West. Opening hours are from 7.30 to 12 am and 1.30 to 5 pm daily.
