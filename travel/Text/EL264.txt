Vietcombank, also known as the Bank for Foreign Trade of Vietnam, occupies two adjacent building at the intersection of Ben Chuong Duong Street and Pasteur. The east building is for foreign exchange and it is worth a visit just to see the stunning ornate interior. It's open 7 to 11.30 am and 1.30 to 3.30 pm daily except Sartuday afternoon and the last day of the month. The bank accepts a wide range of foreign currencies. Travellers cheques in US dollars can be changed for US dollars cash for 1.5% or 2% commision; 3% is the going rate for cash advances on Visa/Master Card.
