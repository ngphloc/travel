Both traditional Vietnamese and Western classical music are performed at the Conservatory of Music, near Reunification Palace are held at 7.30 pm each monday and Friday during the two concert seasons (March-May and October-December).
Students aged seven to 16 attend the conservatory, which performs all the functions of a public school in addtion to providing instruction in music. The music teachers here were trained in Fance, Britain and the USA, as well as the former Eastern Bloc. The school is free, but most of the students come from well-off families who can afford the prerequisite instruments.
